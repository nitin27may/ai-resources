


# AI Governance Operating Model

## Overview

This document outlines the governance structure, bodies, and decision-making frameworks for AI initiatives within the organization.

---

## Governance Bodies & Responsibilities

### AI Ethics Board (Strategic Oversight)

!!! info "Purpose: Strategic Direction and Ethics"
    The AI Ethics Board provides executive-level oversight for AI strategy and ethical considerations.

**Composition:**

- Senior executives and business leaders

**Key Responsibilities:**

- Set AI ethics principles and strategic direction
- Review high-risk AI applications
- Provide final escalation point for critical decisions

**Meeting Cadence:** Quarterly meetings with escalation path

---

### Data Governance Committee (Data Policies)

!!! info "Purpose: Data Management and Privacy"
    The Data Governance Committee ensures proper data handling and compliance with privacy regulations.

**Composition:**

- Data owners
- Privacy officers
- Compliance team

**Key Responsibilities:**

- Define data classification and handling requirements
- Approve data usage for AI applications
- Ensure compliance with data protection regulations

**Meeting Cadence:** Monthly meetings for data-related decisions

---

### Technical Review Board (Architecture & Implementation)

!!! info "Purpose: Technical Excellence and Security"
    The Technical Review Board ensures architectural soundness and security compliance.

**Composition:**

- Enterprise architects
- Security architects
- AI engineers

**Key Responsibilities:**

- Review technical designs and security controls
- Approve architecture patterns and tool selections
- Ensure technical best practices and standards

**Meeting Cadence:** Bi-weekly meetings for technical reviews

---

### Center of Excellence - CoE (Implementation Support)

!!! success "Purpose: Enablement and Best Practices"
    The Center of Excellence provides hands-on support and guidance for AI development teams.

**Composition:**

- AI specialists
- Solution architects
- Engineers

**Key Responsibilities:**

- Provide guidance, best practices, and reusable components
- Support teams throughout AI development lifecycle
- Maintain knowledge base and training materials

**Availability:** Office hours and on-demand consultations

---

## Decision-Making Framework

!!! abstract "Risk-Based Approval Process"
    Decisions are made based on risk level to balance agility with appropriate oversight.

### Approval Levels

| Risk Level | Approval Process | Timeline |
|------------|------------------|----------|
| **Low Risk** | Streamlined approval through CoE | 3-5 days |
| **Medium Risk** | Technical Review Board approval | 1 week |
| **High Risk** | AI Ethics Board review required | 2 weeks |

### Key Principles

- **Risk-based approval**: Low risk = streamlined, High risk = board review
- **Clear escalation paths**: Well-defined routes for complex decisions
- **Defined timelines**: Predictable decision-making timelines
- **Pre-approved patterns**: Common scenarios follow established templates
